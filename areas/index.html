Where we work

Local hedge trimming across East Kent

Sandwich is home. We work the villages and towns within about ten miles of the Quay — from Sandwich Bay on one side to Ash and Woodnesborough on the other.

Our home patch. From Strand Street to the Salutation grounds — if you've got a hedge here, we probably pass it every week.
