Village hedges done right

Old gardens, proper hedges, careful hands

Worth has the kind of gardens where you find mature beech and hornbeam hedges that have been there for decades. The trick with those is restraint, one careful cut in late summer keeps them dense, keeps the coppery winter colour, and keeps the shape the previous gardener spent years building.

We'll happily take on the younger privet round the new-build plots too, mind you. Whatever you've got growing.
