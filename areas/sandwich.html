Based in town, on your road most weeks

Sandwich gardens have their own character. Narrow plots along Strand Street and St Peter's with mature privet hedges out front. Bigger gardens round by the Salutation and out towards the Quay. Newer estates off the Woodnesborough Road with young box and photinia just settling in.

We work all of it. Being in town means we can be round to look at a hedge the same day, most of the time — handy if you've got an estate agent photo coming up or the in-laws arriving on Saturday.
