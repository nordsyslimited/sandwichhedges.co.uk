Questions

The ones we get asked every week

How much does a hedge trim cost?

Honest answer, it depends. A twenty-foot privet running along the front is a very different job to a six-foot leylandii round the back. We quote every hedge after a look or a photo, and we stick to the number. No "extras" on the day.

Will you take the clippings away?

Yes. Always included, never a surprise line on the invoice. Sheets down, rake, final sweep, clippings loaded. You'll get the drive back cleaner than you'd cut it yourself.

When can you actually come?

Most weeks we can be there within 5–7 days. Peak summer gets busier, if you're in a rush, text us a photo and we'll tell you honestly what slot we've got.

Is it too late to cut now?

Rarely. For most species, if it's grown, it can come off. The one we watch is leylandii, cut too hard into bare wood and it won't come back. If you've got a borderline case, we'll tell you before we start.

Are you the same team as Sandwich Lawn Mowing?

Same family, same van. Richard runs both. Hedges got busy enough to deserve their own shop, so here we are.
